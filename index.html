About Sylvan County

Sylvan County is a thoughtfully planned residential community offering 220 stunning homes, designed by the renowned architect Hafeez Contractor. The community consists of 112 apartments and the rest are luxurious bungalows, perfectly suited for those seeking elegance and comfort. Located in the prestigious Mahindra World City, Chennai, Sylvan County offers the best of modern living amidst nature.

Life at Mahindra World City

Mahindra World City is India's first integrated business city, designed to seamlessly blend work, living, and leisure. It offers world-class amenities and facilities, including:

  • The Canopy: A vibrant 60,000 sq. ft. shopping and lifestyle center with retail stores, a supermarket, food court, and more.
  • MWC Club: A recreational hub with a gymnasium, swimming pool, and sports facilities.
  • Healthcare Facilities: Modern medical centers and hospitals for quality healthcare.
  • Special Economic Zones (SEZs): Dedicated zones catering to various industries, fostering economic growth.
  • Mahindra World School: A state-of-the-art educational institution offering excellent facilities like specialized laboratories, a library, and a vast playfield.

Celebrating Hafeez Contractor

The Visionary Architect

Hafeez Sorab Contractor, born in 1950, is a distinguished Indian architect known for transforming urban skylines. With a degree from the University of Mumbai and a master's from Columbia University, he has designed iconic structures like The Imperial Towers in Mumbai and The 42 in Kolkata. Awarded the Padma Bhushan in 2016, his work blends innovation and functionality, redefining modern architecture.
