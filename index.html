About Sylvan County

Sylvan County is a thoughtfully planned residential community offering 220 stunning homes, designed by the renowned architect Hafeez Contractor. The community consists of 112 apartments and the rest are luxurious bungalows, perfectly suited for those seeking elegance and comfort.
